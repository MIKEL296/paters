Our Story

Founded on Faith, Built on Community, Focused on Jesus

How It All Began

Pathers Church was founded in 2010 with a simple vision: to create a welcoming community where people could experience authentic faith, genuine community, and the transformative power of Jesus Christ.

What started as a small gathering of 30 people in a rented community center has grown into a vibrant faith community of over 2,000 members. But our growth has never changed our core identity‚Äîwe remain a place where every person is valued, every voice is heard, and every soul matters to God.

Our story is your story. We're not just a church building; we're a movement of believers committed to following Jesus and making a difference in the world. Whether you're taking your first step of faith or you've been walking with God for decades, there's a place for you here at Pathers.
